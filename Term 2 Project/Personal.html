Personal Connections

I love Dragon Ball Kakarot because when I was growing up in Hong Kong, I'd watch their shows and all of their series on TV and I fell in love with the anime.Ever since then, I'd bought a lot of dragon ball games and whenever I did, I never felt satisfied with the game because it wouldn't be necessarily a re-live of Goku's story. So when Dragon Ball Kakarot came out I was really happy that a game like that came out because it re-lived Goku's story and it relived my memories as a child watching so much Dragon Ball.
